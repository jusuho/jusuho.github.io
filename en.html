Turning Ambiguous Requirements into Reliable Engineering Systems

I'm JuSuHo, currently serving as Head of Technology at Taiwan's largest chain of adult day care centers, and a graduate of the National Taiwan University Graduate Institute of Computer Science and Information Engineering.

Over the past 10 years, my work has spanned AI transformation, business decision-making, full-stack development, systems integration, project management, cloud architecture, and engineering team building. I don't just build systems—I establish the processes, quality standards, collaboration norms, technical decision frameworks, and governance mechanisms that make them sustainable.

I've been continuously integrating AI across every layer of the organization—including the team's daily development workflow—making code generation, refactoring, testing, and knowledge transfer more leveraged. I don't treat AI as a buzzword; I treat it as a practical tool for improving business profitability and delivery velocity.
